Our Da'wah Signpost Project In our continued effort to revive and propagate the Sunnah across Ebiraland, the United Muslim Sisters of Kogi Central (UMS-KGC) initiated a Dawah Signpost Project. These signposts are silent reminders strategically placed in public spaces to reconnect hearts with the words of Allah.

UMS-KGC Beauty Lounge - A Vision for Halal Self-Care The UMS-KGC Beauty Lounge was born to make self-care accessible, comfortable, and halal for Muslim women. Due to financial challenges and low turnout, we paused operations. We remain hopeful to revive this initiative with support.

UMS-KGC Ramadan Feeding Project Every Ramadan, we provide food and support to needy families, widows, and vulnerable individuals through our annual Ramadan Feeding Project.
