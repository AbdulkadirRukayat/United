Welcome to UMS-KGC Official Homepage

Founded on February 16, 2018, UMS-KGC is a faith-based organization committed to empowering Muslim women in Ebira land through Islamic Education, community development, and spiritual enrichment. Our goal is to promote Islamic values, revive the Sunnah, and foster a supportive community of Muslim sisters.

Our Mission

To promote Islamic values and foster community among Ebira Muslim women through various projects, programs, and spiritual guidance rooted in the teachings of the Qur'an and Sunnah.

Our Vision

To be a leading Islamic organization dedicated to nurturing a global community of knowledgeable, empowered, and compassionate Muslim sisters committed to uplifting society and strengthening the Ummah.

Goals

  • Enhance Islamic Knowledge and practice among members
  • Provide support and assistance to those in need
  • Foster sisterhood and community
  • Achieve financial stability
